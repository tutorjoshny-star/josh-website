About

I'm a Long Island native and Salutatorian of Half Hollow Hills High School. I earned both my B.S. and M.S. in engineering from the University of Pennsylvania — graduating summa cum laude and completing both degrees in just four years. I scored in the 99th percentile on both the SAT and ACT, and have worked with hundreds of students across math and test prep.

My engineering background shapes the way I teach — thoughtful, systematic, and focused on helping students truly understand how and why ideas work. Rather than relying on memorization, I guide students in building clear frameworks they can apply across math and standardized testing.

What I find most rewarding is working closely with students to identify where they're getting stuck and helping them build genuine confidence through steady, measurable progress.
